The Lost Amulet

Chapter 3

You decide to venture into the cave alone, relying on your own skills and wit to navigate its dangers. You cautiously make your way through the twisting passages, avoiding pitfalls and traps along the way. Eventually, you reach a chamber where the Lost Amulet rests upon a pedestal. As you reach out to claim it, a hidden mechanism triggers, sealing you inside the chamber forever. You failed to anticipate the final trap and die in the cave.
