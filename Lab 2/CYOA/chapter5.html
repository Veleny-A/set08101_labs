The Lost Amulet

Chapter 2.1

You take the gun you hid in your backpack and shoot your companion. Once you know he is dead you hide his body under some rocks. You cover the Lost Amulet in a cloth and place it in your bag and then leave to go home. When you reach home, you try your best to sell the Lost Amulet on the dark web to earn money for your brother's cancer operation.

The END.
